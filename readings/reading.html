my reading response 3

Sina Bahram’s introduction with his own blindness allows the audience to understand his perspective even more. The main point that Bahram is making is that there is always a “separate” interface to adhere to any users that do not fit with the target audience. Merging experiences so that users that are inhibited do not have to suffer by being unable to participate in “normal” interfaces that everyone else is able to participate in. For me, it makes me feel that we should do this already by creating things that are tolerant of all other aspects, giving into the assumption that “anything could go wrong”. Users will do a lot of things that are beyond what you envisioned and as a designer it should be their duty to create things that are tolerant of subjection rather than restricting the possible actions that users may do.

my reading response 4

Algorithms help us to solve issues at a rapid pace to give users instant gratification in their efforts to have a resolve to whatever particular situation. These algorithms are the subject their creators intellect and opinion of “right” and “wrong” and their swift justice can be misled because of the input that has been embedded into them which gives algorithms the ability to recognize situations as negative or positive. This input could be beneficial to some and could handicap others depending upon the situation. Algorithms should be used as extensions used to disperse of minute problems that can be settled easily rather than as a blanket coat for everything. The idea of having code and machines be the main resolver for situations such as racial, sexual and prejudice abuse is absurd. Algorithms can help pin point serious issues dealing with race and inequality but to give them to responsibility of resolving this is impossible for they are preprogrammed with opinions and prejudices of their creators. To protect themselves online, users have begun to mass together and block or report abuse to help raise awareness. This should be taken into account with algorithms for it allows them to find serious issues to hand off to a human being much more efficiently rather than a case by case assembly line.
